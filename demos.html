--- layout: page --- WEB UNDER CONSTRUCTION The circle of life full montage:

The Circle of life is a song from Disney's Lion King film. We adapted it to a 6-voice a cappella montage thanks to Diana Ferrando, an amateur singer. The track is composed by 1 leading voice (rafiki, bottom center), 4 voices which usually sing the same in pairs, one voice which emulates a backtrack and a base of percussion instruments. This is a challenging scenario as all the voices are performed by the same singer.
